About

Hi there! My name is Denisse Lopez (Denny). I am a recent graduate from UC Santa Barbara, where I earned a Bachelor’s in Spanish Language and a minor in Translation Studies. I’m fluent in English and Spanish, conversational in French, and still working on my Portuguese, Italian and ASL. I moved to Kirkland this summer (2019) and I live with my boyfriend Jason, and I would love to take care of your kids!

I have been caring for children since I was 16 years old and I moved to CA from Mexico. I’ve helped boys and girls ages < 2 to 13 grow and thrive… in Enlgish and Spanish. Whether you need regular daycare, pick up and drop off from school, help around the house, or someone to watch the kids on date night… I’m your girl! If you would like to get in touch or set up an interview, please fill out the contact form below.

Friedman-Husselman Family

I am writing to recommend Denisse Lopez as a babysitter. We have known Denisse for the past 6 years. Over the years she has cared for our children on many occasions. My daughter is now 16 and my son is 10, Denisse began babysitting for us 6 years ago when they were both younger. Denisse is by far the best babysitter we've ever had. She is kind, fun, funny and loving with children. She is able to care for kids in a way that makes them feel special and loved. She is also extremely responsible and can be trusted to feed, bathe, play with, entertain, dress and put kids to bed. We've had Denisse care for our children over night as well many times. On top of her fabulous babysitting skills, Denisse does a great job cleaning up and we've come to refer to a SUPER clean kitchen as "Deni clean" because she is so thorough in her cleaning. Deni is also completely bilingual in Spanish and English. While she is a native speaker of Spanish, she also speaks English with native speaker fluency. We have watched Denisse on her path, finishing her degree from UC Santa Barbara and we are endlessly impressed with her drive, academic skills, people skills and joy for life. I would recommend Denisse for babysitting jobs of children of ANY age (she's worked with infants and babies and was an excellent babysitter for our young son and for our teenage daughter). Any family would be LUCKY to bring Denisse into their life as she has so much to give. We feel fortunate that she's been part of our lives for many, many years now.
-Tamara Friedman
